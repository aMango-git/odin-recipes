Japanese Milk Bread (Shokupan)

Photo: Provided by chopstickchronicles.com


Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup of Tang Zhong roux
  • 200g Bread flour
  • 150ml Milk
  • 15g sugar
  • 3g Instant dry yeast
  • 10g Unsalted butter
  • 5g Salt

Steps

  1. Make Yudane the night before. (8-12 hours)
  2. Knead all ingredients using a stand mixer, bread machine or by hand (20min)
  3. The first rise (45-60 min depends on the temperature)
  4. Divide and roll
  5. Bench time (20 mins)
  6. Shape
  7. The second rise (30 mins)
  8. Bake for 30 mins at 185 degrees celcius
